Stay Safe Online

Online Safety Tips

Protect yourself with these essential safety practices

Protect Your Privacy
  • Review privacy settings on all social media
  • Never share your location publicly
  • Use strong, unique passwords
  • Enable two-factor authentication
Document Everything
  • Screenshot all threatening messages
  • Save dates, times, and usernames
  • Don't delete evidence
  • Keep a written log of incidents
Block & Report
  • Block harassers immediately
  • Report abuse to platforms
  • Don't engage with trolls
  • Inform trusted friends/family
Know Your Resources
  • Save local emergency numbers
  • Know your nearest GBV helpline
  • Identify safe people to contact
  • Use Project Imara for support
Take Care of Yourself
  • It's not your fault
  • Seek emotional support
  • Take breaks from social media
  • Consider talking to a counselor
Emergency Actions
  • If in immediate danger, call police
  • Go to a safe location
  • Tell someone you trust
  • Use Imara to report & get help
