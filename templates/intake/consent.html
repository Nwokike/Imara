User Consent & Data Protection

Your data rights and our privacy practices

Your Privacy Matters

Imara is designed with survivor safety as the top priority. We understand that reporting abuse can put you at risk, which is why we've built our platform with privacy-first principles.

What Data We Collect
  • Report Content: Text, images, voice notes, and documents you submit
  • Optional Email: Only if you want to receive confirmation and updates
  • Location Information: Only if you provide it, to connect you with local authorities
  • Platform Identifier: Your Telegram ID or web session (not tied to real identity)
How We Use Your Data
  • AI Analysis: To assess threat levels and provide appropriate advice
  • Case Documentation: Create forensic-grade evidence with SHA-256 hashing
  • Authority Notifications: Only for high-risk cases, and only with your consent
  • Service Improvement: Anonymized, aggregated data to improve our AI models
Data Protection Measures
Encryption

All data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest (AES-256)

Hash Verification

SHA-256 hashes ensure evidence integrity for legal proceedings

Zero-UI Design

No app installation required - leaves no trace on your device

Secure Storage

Data stored on secure cloud infrastructure with access controls

When We Share Data

We only share your data in these circumstances:

  • High-Risk Cases: When AI detects imminent danger (score 7+), we notify relevant authorities
  • Your Request: If you explicitly request escalation to authorities
  • Legal Requirement: Where required by law for criminal proceedings
Note: You can always say "IMARA STOP" to cancel a report before it's submitted.
Your Rights
  • Access: Request a copy of data we hold about you
  • Correction: Update incorrect information
  • Deletion: Request deletion (subject to legal requirements)
  • Portability: Receive your data in a standard format
Contact Us

For privacy-related inquiries, please contact us.
Response Time: Within 30 days
