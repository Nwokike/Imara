Terms of Service

Rules for using Imara and our reporting service

Report Processing Flow

When you submit a report to Imara, it goes through a structured analysis and response process:

1

Submission

Report received via Telegram or Web portal. Evidence is immediately hashed for integrity.

2

Smart Analysis

Multi-modal AI analyzes text, images, and audio to assess threat level.

3

Triage Decision

Based on risk score: low (advice) or high (automatic escalation to support partners).

4

Response

User receives immediate feedback. High-risk cases get partner notification.

Risk Score Thresholds
Score Level Response
1-3 Low Risk Safety advice provided, no escalation
4-6 Moderate Risk Detailed safety guidance, optional escalation
7-10 High Risk Automatic notification to support partners with evidence
Partner Escalation

When a high-risk case is detected, we:

  • Identify the appropriate support partner based on user's location
  • Send a secure forensic alert via email within minutes
  • Include: Case ID, risk assessment, evidence hashes, and summary
  • Do NOT share your personal contact information unless you opt-in
Data Retention
Data Type Retention Period
Case records (high-risk) 7 years (legal requirements)
Case records (low-risk) 1 year
Chat session data 30 days after last interaction
Uploaded files As per case retention
False Reports

We take false reporting seriously:

  • Deliberately false reports may result in service suspension
  • We may share information with law enforcement if false reports cause harm
  • AI safeguards help detect potentially fabricated content
Report Cancellation

You can cancel a report at any time before final submission:

  • Telegram: Say "IMARA STOP", "STOP", or "CANCEL"
  • Web: Close the browser before clicking Submit
Note: Once a report is submitted and escalated, it cannot be fully recalled as partners may have already been notified.

For policy questions, please contact us.
